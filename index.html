NYC DangerZone

Breakdown of Seven Major Crime Indexes Throughout Five Boroughs in 2015

Murder, Assault, Rape, Robbery, Burglary, Grand Larceny, Grand Larceny of Motor Vehichle

You are approximately 70% more likely to be murdered in Brooklyn than Manhattan and nearly twice as likely to be assaulted.



The Bronx and Manhattan have nearly the same amount of Burglaries, while Brooklyn had nearly as many robberies as Manhattan and Bronx combined.


Queens ans Staten Island are the only places where burglaries occured more that robberies. In fact, Queens is second only to Brooklyn in number of burglaries.


Occurrences of rape are steady among the Bronx, Queens and Manhattan. They peak in Brooklyn and happened the least in Staten Island



Grand Larceny was the most committed crime in 2015. There were twice as many occurrences of grand larceny (31,665) as the second most committed crime, assault (15,056). In fact while murder grand larceny has remained steady. Assault is also up slightly from 2004 and both crimes are on the rise since 2010.This crime is significantly more frequent in Manhattan.
